# Off-Page SEO & Link Building Strategy

## 3-Step Link Building Approach

### Step 1: Foundation Building (Month 1-2)

#### A. Local Educational Partnerships
1. **Target Universities & Colleges**:
   - Tribhuvan University departments
   - Kathmandu University resource pages
   - Medical colleges in Nepal
   - Engineering colleges
   - Create valuable resources they'd want to link to

2. **Student Organizations**:
   - Nepal Medical Students' Society
   - Engineering Students' Association Nepal
   - Science clubs and forums
   - Offer free workshops/webinars

3. **Educational Directories**:
   ```
   - Nepal Education Directory
   - South Asian Education Resources
   - Medical Education Portals
   - Engineering Education Databases
   ```

#### B. Resource Link Building
1. **Create Linkable Assets**:
   - Comprehensive syllabus PDFs (downloadable)
   - Interactive exam calculators
   - Infographic: "Nepal Education System Overview"
   - Free study planners and templates
   - Previous year paper compilations

2. **Scholarship Pages**:
   - Create scholarship database for Nepali students
   - Partner with scholarship providers
   - Natural backlink opportunities

### Step 2: Content-Driven Outreach (Month 3-4)

#### A. Guest Posting Strategy
1. **Target Publications**:
   ```markdown
   Tier 1 (High Authority):
   - The Himalayan Times Education Section
   - Republica Education Portal
   - Nepal Education News
   - MyRepublica Campus
   
   Tier 2 (Niche Relevant):
   - Medical education blogs
   - Engineering career websites
   - Study abroad consultancies
   - Career counseling platforms
   
   Tier 3 (Student Platforms):
   - Student forums
   - College magazines
   - Youth publications
   ```

2. **Guest Post Topics**:
   - "Complete Guide to Medical Education in Nepal"
   - "Engineering Entrance Exams: IOE vs TU vs KU"
   - "How Technology is Changing Entrance Exam Preparation"
   - "Success Stories: From Rural Nepal to Medical College"

#### B. Expert Roundups & Interviews
1. **Interview Targets**:
   - Medical Education Commission officials
   - University professors
   - Successful entrance exam toppers
   - Career counselors

2. **Roundup Topics**:
   - "10 Educators Share Their Best Entrance Exam Tips"
   - "Medical Professionals Advice for MECEE-BL Aspirants"
   - "Engineering Deans Discuss Future of Technical Education"

```
[Resource Name] - [Brief description and unique value]

Entrance Academy
```

```
Best,
[Your name]
```

## Competitive Link Analysis

### Competitor Backlink Audit:
1. **Identify Top Competitors**:
   - Local: Nepal exam prep sites
   - Regional: South Asian platforms
   - International: Global test prep

2. **Link Gap Analysis**:
   - Find sites linking to competitors but not you
   - Identify competitor's best content
   - Analyze their link building tactics
   - Replicate and improve strategies

3. **Monthly Competitor Tracking**:
   - New links acquired
   - Content that earned links
   - Social media growth
   - PR mentions and coverage
